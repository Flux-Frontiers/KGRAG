# Logo Design Brief — Flux-Frontiers KG Suite

*Covers: KGRAG, GutenbergKG, MetaKG, DiaryKG*

---

## Unified Brand System

### Design Language (shared across all logos)

**Metaphor**: Knowledge as a living graph — nodes of light connected by edges, structure emerging from raw text.

**Shape vocabulary**: Circular/hexagonal nodes, clean edge lines, hub-and-spoke topology. No skeuomorphic clutter.

**Typeface**: Geometric sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Neue Haas Grotesk, or similar). Name in medium weight, tagline in light weight.

**Color palette**:

| Role | Value | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Deep navy | `#0F1B2D` | Backgrounds, primary mark |
| Gold/amber | `#F5A623` | Primary accent, "knowledge" nodes |
| Ice blue | `#7EC8E3` | Secondary graph edges |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Text, negative space |

**Logo format**: Square mark (for GitHub avatar/badge) + horizontal lockup (mark + wordmark). Must work at 32px and 512px.

## KGRAG

**Concept**: *The Federation Hub* — multiple distinct subgraphs converging into a single luminous central node, representing unified orchestration across domains.

**Visual description**:
- Center: one large bright gold node (the orchestration hub)
- Surrounding it: 5–6 smaller nodes in distinct muted colors (code=teal, docs=blue, metabolic=green, memory=purple, legal=orange) — each trailing a small mini-cluster of 2–3 nodes behind it
- All nodes connected by thin clean lines to the central hub
- The overall silhouette fits inside a circle or hexagon
- Background: deep navy `#0F1B2D`
- The constellation pattern reads as a single unified star map at small sizes

**Wordmark**: `KGRAG` in geometric sans, all-caps, medium weight.
**Tagline** (optional): *Federated Knowledge, One Query.*

## GutenbergKG

**Concept**: *The Living Book* — an open book whose pages dissolve into a graph network, representing literature made queryable.

**Visual description**:
- Left half: stylized open book with 2–3 visible text lines (suggesting a manuscript or printed page)
- Right half / emanating from the spine: the text lines transition into graph nodes and edges flowing outward — same node style as KGRAG (circles + lines)
- The book's spine is vertical, centered; left = analog, right = digital graph

- Background: deep navy `#0F1B2D` (same as KGRAG)
- At small sizes: reads as a book-with-dot-trail, unambiguously literary + technical

**Wordmark**: `GutenbergKG` in the same geometric sans, title-case.

## What Ties Them Together

- Same dark navy background
- Same node/edge visual grammar (nodes are always circles, edges are always thin straight lines)
- Same typeface and weight system
- Gold accent node always present as the "knowledge" color
- Marks are self-contained but clearly family-related

## MetaboKG

**Concept**: *The Pathway Map* — a metabolic reaction network rendered as a graph, evoking the iconic circular pathway diagrams of biochemistry but abstracted into the KG suite's visual language.

**Visual description**:
- A ring of medium nodes in biological greens (`#4CAF50`, `#81C784`) and warm coral (`#EF9A9A`) representing compounds and enzymes respectively
- Directional edges (slightly thicker than in KGRAG) connecting them in a closed-loop cycle suggesting a metabolic pathway (glycolysis, TCA, etc.)
- One node in the ring glows gold `#F5A623` — the focal compound (e.g., ATP or glucose) — as the KG suite's signature accent
- Small arrowheads on edges to convey reaction directionality
- Background: deep navy `#0F1B2D`
- Overall shape: organic circular/elliptical, not the rigid hexagon of KGRAG

**Wordmark**: `MetaKG` in geometric sans, title-case, medium weight.
**Tagline** (optional): *Metabolism as a Queryable Graph.*

## DiaryKG

**Concept**: *The Remembered Moment* — a journal page whose handwritten lines transform into a temporal graph, representing personal memory made searchable and structured over time.

**Visual description**:
- Left side: the silhouette of an open journal/notebook, slightly tilted, with 3–4 thin horizontal lines suggesting handwriting — rendered in warm white/cream on navy
- From the rightmost lines, the strokes dissolve into a sparse timeline: a horizontal axis with 3–4 vertical tick marks (representing dates/entries), each tick sprouting a small gold circle node above it
- The gold nodes are connected by a thin horizontal line (the timeline edge) in ice blue `#7EC8E3`
- Optional: faint SIMILAR_TO arcs connecting non-adjacent nodes, suggesting cross-entry memory links
- Background: deep navy `#0F1B2D`
- Mood: intimate and contemplative, not corporate — softer than the other marks

**Wordmark**: `DiaryKG` in geometric sans, title-case, medium weight.
**Tagline** (optional): *Every Entry, Remembered.*

## Generation Tips

- **Midjourney**: Use `V` (variation) on the best result, then `U` to upscale. Add `--no text, letters, words` if it renders the name inside the mark.
- **DALL-E 3**: If unwanted text appears inside the mark, append *"no letters, no text, no words inside the image"* to the prompt.
- **SVG export**: Trace the raster result in Figma (Plugins → Vectorize) or Inkscape (Path → Trace Bitmap) to get a clean SVG for README badge sizes.
